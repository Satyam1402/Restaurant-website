## Common Questions

### How do I place an order?
Browse our menu, add items to cart, proceed to checkout, enter delivery details, and complete payment.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards and digital payment methods during checkout.

### How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary by location and order complexity. Estimated time is shown at checkout and on order confirmation.

### Can I modify my order after placing it?
Contact us immediately after placing your order. We'll do our best to accommodate changes if the order hasn't been prepared yet.

### Do you offer vegetarian/vegan options?
Yes! We have multiple vegetarian and vegan options clearly marked on our menu. Use the dietary filter to find them easily.

### What if I have food allergies?
All menu items list their ingredients. For specific allergy concerns, please contact us before ordering.

### How do I track my order?
After placing an order, visit the Orders page (/orders) to track your order status in real-time.

### Can I reorder previous items?
Yes! View your order history at /orders and easily reorder your favorite meals.

### Do you have gluten-free options?
Yes, we offer several gluten-free options across all menu categories, clearly marked on each item.

### What are your spice levels?
We rate spice levels from 1-5, with 1 being mild and 5 being very spicy. Check each item for its spice rating.
